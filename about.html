2nd Year College Students | E-Commerce Project

Hello! We're Denmark Osano and Wayne Sulleza, 2nd-year college students, and this is our e-commerce website project for anime figurines. We created AkibaFigures as part of our coursework to demonstrate our skills in web development, e-commerce functionality, and user experience design.

This website showcases a collection of premium anime figurines from popular series including Naruto, One Piece, Demon Slayer, Jujutsu Kaisen, and more. We've implemented features such as:

  • Product catalog with categories and filtering
  • Shopping cart functionality with local storage
  • User authentication pages (signup/login)
  • Product detail pages with image galleries
  • Responsive design that works on all devices
  • Search functionality for finding figures

"This project represents our passion for both anime and web development. We hope you enjoy exploring our e-commerce site!" - Denmark & Wayne
